Specifications for generating a family-safe coloring book:

- **Total Pages:** 10 coloring pages.
- **Content:** Family safe themes, specifically focusing on dinosaurs and sharks.
- **Coloring Page Style:** Black and white with thick lines. No text on coloring pages.
- **Cover Image:** Full color, full bleed cover. Can include any text, perhaps the title "Dino and Shark Coloring Fun!"
- **Watermark:** A readable "fairypaint.com" watermark *within the images directly* on *every* page (cover and coloring pages). The watermark should look like smeared purple paint.
- **Bumblebee Inclusion:** Each coloring page must include a photorealistic, life-size bumblebee *within the images directly* somewhere on the page.
