Specifications for generating a family-safe coloring book:

- **Total Pages:** 12 coloring pages.
- **Content:** Family safe themes.
- **Coloring Page Style:** Black and white with thick lines. No text on coloring pages.
- **Cover Image:** Full color, full bleed cover. Can include any text.
- **Watermark:** A readable "fairypaint.com" watermark on *every* page (cover and coloring pages). The watermark should look like smeared purple paint.
- **Bumblebee and Butterfly Inclusion:** Each coloring page must include a photorealistic, life-size bumblebee and a photorealistic, life-size butterfly somewhere on the page.
